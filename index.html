Meet Your DJ

"It's Storm Season!"

There has been one constant in my life and that is my love for music and connecting with people through it. DJing will never be a job for me, always a passion. My passion for music really began in highschool (2003) when I began recording/producing music with several of my friends. We started recording utilizing our headphones in the microphone jack with sound recorder as our recording program (old heads will know about that!) and over time upgraded to studio microphones and cool edit pro/adobe audition.

I began my DJ career in 2018. Since then I have provided music and entertainment for many weddings, birthday/anniversary parties, school/market/sporting events, etc. One of my greatest strengths of being a DJ is my catelog of music and experience at different venue types. I have all music from Rap, R&B, Reggae and Southern Soul all the way to 80s Rock, Country and Beach Music. I've played at many event locations in the eastern NC and VA area. To name a few, NC Wesleyan College - Rocky Mount NC, Seven Springs Vineyards - Norlina NC, Weldon Mills & Dic=stillery - Weldon NC, Shady Shack Bar & Grill - Bracey VA, National Black Farmers Association - Baskerville, VA and just recently The Pointe @ Lake Gaston NC.


I also believe in supporting the local artist! I've worked on my first mixtape called "Storm Watch: 252 Artist Spotlight Vol. 1". It features some of the hottest artist from the 252 area. Contact me if you would like a chance to be on the next one!
